SEVENTEEN

SEVENTEEN is a South Korean boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment, debuting on May 26, 2015, with their extended play 17 Carat. Known for their self-producing abilities, the group is composed of 13 members: S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Woozi, DK, Mingyu, The8, Seungkwan, Vernon, and Dino. SEVENTEEN is divided into three subunits: hip-hop, vocal, and performance, showcasing their versatility in music and performances. They gained popularity with hits like "Adore U," "Don't Wanna Cry," and "Very Nice," and are known for their intricate choreography and self-produced music. SEVENTEEN’s fanbase is called Carat, and their official color is Rose Quartz.
