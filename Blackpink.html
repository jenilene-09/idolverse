BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, debuting on August 8, 2016, with the single album Square One, featuring hits like "Boombayah" and "Whistle." The group consists of four members: Jisoo (lead vocalist and visual), Jennie (main rapper and sub-vocalist), Rosé (main vocalist), and Lisa (main dancer and lead rapper). Known for their powerful performances, versatile music blending pop, hip hop, and EDM, and global influence, BLACKPINK quickly became one of the most successful K-pop acts, breaking records on YouTube, performing at Coachella, and winning multiple prestigious awards.
